## Domaine La Loingtaine

Domaine La Loingtaine includes a dwelling house, apartments, a concert hall, and a park on the edge of the river Loing. It has been a centre for concerts and artistic activities since 2005.

### Main Activities

- **Musical program production** — Direction: Aki Saulière and Raphael Bell
- **Research and development** — Direction: Masako Saulière
- **Musician accommodation** — Owned by Claude Saulière

### Association Schubertiades

Created in 2005, the Association Schubertiades was established to organize high-quality chamber music concerts and support resident artists at La Loingtaine.

### Our Impact

Over 200 concerts have been organized since 2005. La Loingtaine has evolved into a place of retreat, artistic creation, and cultural richness — a unique venue where musicians and audiences come together in an intimate, inspiring setting.

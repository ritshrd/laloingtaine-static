## The Site

La Loingtaine is an 18th-century farmhouse, lovingly renovated in the early 1900s, nestled near the magnificent forest of Fontainebleau in the village of Montigny-sur-Loing.

## A Place of Peace and Creation

The peaceful setting — with its park, old trees, and the gentle flow of the Loing river — encourages both contemplation and creation. It is a space where art and nature intertwine, where the rhythm of the seasons accompanies the rhythm of music.

## A Borderless Space

La Loingtaine has become a "borderless space" for intercultural dialogue, where artists and specialists from around the world can perform, rest, reflect, and teach. It is a unique venue where the intimacy of chamber music finds its natural home.

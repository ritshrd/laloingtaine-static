La Loingtaine is located near the beautiful forest of Fontainebleau, in the village of Montigny-sur-Loing.

## 📍 Address

**La Loingtaine**  
40 rue Grande, Sorques  
77690 Montigny sur Loing, France

## 🚆 By Train

From Paris, take the train from **Gare de Lyon** towards **Moret-Veneux-les-Sablons** or **Montigny-sur-Loing**. The journey takes approximately 1 hour. La Loingtaine is a short walk from the station.

## 🚗 By Car

From Paris, take the **A6 motorway** towards Lyon/Fontainebleau. Exit at **Fontainebleau** and follow signs to Montigny-sur-Loing. Approximately 1 hour 15 minutes from central Paris.

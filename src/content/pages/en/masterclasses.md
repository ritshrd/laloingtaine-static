Monthly opportunities for conservatory students to develop their musical identity under the guidance of Aki Saulière.

The masterclasses at La Loingtaine offer a unique opportunity for young musicians to work on developing their **personal musical identity**. Beyond technique, students explore interpretation, phrasing, and the deeper qualities that transform a performance into a profound musical experience.

Led by **Aki Saulière**, these monthly sessions provide individual attention in an encouraging and supportive environment. Students perform for their peers, receive detailed feedback, and gain insights that go beyond what traditional conservatory training can offer.

The La Loingtaine String Academy takes place each summer in late July and August, offering an immersive musical experience in the heart of the French countryside.

## String Academy 2025

The academy provides an intensive yet nurturing environment where students work closely with internationally acclaimed professors on solo and chamber music repertoire.

Students benefit from daily lessons, ensemble coaching, performance opportunities, and the unique experience of living and making music alongside established professionals.

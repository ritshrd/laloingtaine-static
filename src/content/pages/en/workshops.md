Orchestral study for auditions, led by experienced orchestra members following the traditions of maestros like Abbado and Harnoncourt.

The workshops at La Loingtaine focus on **orchestral repertoire study**, providing essential preparation for young musicians who aspire to join professional orchestras.

Led by experienced orchestra members who carry the performance traditions of great maestros like **Claudio Abbado** and **Nikolaus Harnoncourt**, these workshops bridge the gap between conservatory training and the demands of professional orchestral life.

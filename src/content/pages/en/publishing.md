Since its inception, we have at La Loingtaine met with musicians, writers, and painters who attended our activities. Among these, a fair number turns out to have a second passion be it for drawing, painting, or long-term research. Most often these projects – drawings, paintings, short stories for children, or learned writings of erudite knowledge – cannot command the commercial interest of publishing houses even though they well deserve to be taken out of the drawers and shared more widely.

 

To offer such passionate creators an opening, we decided to create an editorial activity and have founded the La Loingtaine Publishing House in Japan in 2018 as an international publishing house that will publish works in Japanese, English, and French. Besides bringing to light the works of unknown authors La Loingtaine Publishing will strive to identify and publish high quality doctoral theses, and to commission little-known but important case studies within our sphere of interest.

An international publishing house founded in 2018 in Japan, publishing musicians, 
writers, and painters.

**La Loingtaine Publishing** extends our cultural mission to the printed page. 
Founded in 2018, the publishing house bridges the worlds of East and West, bringing 
together voices from across disciplines and cultures.

Our publications include works by musicians who share their artistic insights, 
writers whose stories reflect the human experience, and painters whose visual 
narratives complement our musical mission.

La Loingtaine’s activities are intended to highlight and disseminate knowledge and experience related to music, art, history, and philosophy, through various media. Special attention will be given to cultural knowledge that is lesser known and perhaps in danger of disappearing.

To address this issue, we have launched activities dedicated to the documentation and transmission of knowledge in the form of written materials and books through the establishment of the La Loingtaine Publishing House and the La Loingtaine Virtual Library Project.

## Evolution Since 2005

When La Loingtaine commenced its activities in 2005, the focus was firmly on chamber music. Over the years, however, the scope has widened considerably, evolving into what can be described as an institute for human development.

## Inspired by "Learning to Be"

Inspired by Edgar Faure's landmark report "Learning to Be" (UNESCO, 1972), La Loingtaine now embraces a broader vision that encompasses music, science, culture, and nature — creating a space where different disciplines can interact and enrich one another.

## A New Design for a New Era

This new site architecture aims to reflect the openness and innovation that have come to define La Loingtaine, incorporating inputs from the vibrant community that has emerged around the program. The design reflects our evolution while preserving the intimate, warm character that makes La Loingtaine unique.
